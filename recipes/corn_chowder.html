Corn Chowder

Debra Elliott

Description

Just like Mom makes

Ingredients

  • 3 slices of bacon, coarsely chopped
  • 1 tbsp olive oil
  • 1 medium onion, peeled and cut into 1/4 inch dice
  • 2 ribs of celery, cut into 1/4 inch dice
  • 1 large red pepper, seeded, cored, and cut into 1/4 inch dice
  • 2 medium potatoes, peeled and cut into 1/4 inch dice
  • 4 cups low-sodium chicken broth
  • 1 bay leaf
  • 1/2 tsp dried thyme
  • 1/2 tsp dried dill
  • 4 cups frozen corn kernels
  • 2 cups light cream or half-and-half
  • 1 tsp salt
  • Ground black pepper to taste
  • 1 tbsp cornstarch and 1 tbsp cold water (to thicken, optional)

Steps

  1. In a large soup pot, fry the bacon until crisp in olive oil, about 5 minutes.
  2. Add the onion, celery, and red pepper. Saute over medium-high heat, stirring frequently, until vegetables soften, about 5 minutes.
  3. Add potatoes, broth, and herbs, and simmer about 15 minutes, adjusting heat if necessary so that liquid does not boil.
  4. Stir in corn, cream, salt, and pepper, and simmer over low heat until corn is cooked.
  5. If you want to thicken soup, stir in cornstarch mixture and cook for 1 minute.
  6. Ladle into bowls and serve immediately. May garnish with chopped fresh parsley and a pat of butter if desired.
